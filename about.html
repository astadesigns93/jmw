Artist statment

Janessa M Webb is a detail-oriented artist based in Litchfield County, CT, whose medium of choice is graphic design. With a background in business administration and a focus in marketing, she brings a unique blend of creativity and strategic thinking to her work.

Experienced in utilizing design principles and software tools, Janessa is able to deliver high-quality design output that perfectly captures the essence of her clients' visions.

As an artist, she is passionate about leveraging her creativity to create visually compelling and impactful designs that leave a lasting impression.

Each piece she creates is a unique reflection of her dedication to her craft and her unwavering commitment to delivering excellence.
